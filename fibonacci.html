Fibonacci Sequence

Fibonacci sequence is a sequence in which each element is the sum of the two elements that precede it. Numbers that are part of the Fibonacci sequence are known as Fibonacci numbers, commonly denoted \( F_n \).
It starts with either 1, 1 or with 0, 1

It appears in many natural patterns, like the arrangement of leaves, flowers, shells etc.

The Fibonacci numbers may be defined by the recurrence relation

\[ \qquad \qquad F_1 = F_2 = 1 \qquad \quad (or \; F_0 = 0, \; F_1 = 1) \] \[ F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} \qquad \forall n \gt 2 \]
