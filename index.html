Number Sequence

A number sequence is an ordered list of numbers that follows a specific pattern or rule.
Each number in the sequence is called a term, and the term in position n is usually written as \(\displaystyle a_n\).

Sequence can be represented as:

\[ a_1,\, a_2,\, a_3,\, \dots,\, a_i,\, a_{i+1},\, ... \qquad \forall i \ge 1 \]

where \( a_i \) is \( i^{\text{th}} \) term
